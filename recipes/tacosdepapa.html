Tacos de Papa!

An easy recipe to make tacos at home!

Ingredients

  • 1 package of instant mashed potatoes.
  • 1 diced onion.
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil.
  • Salt and pepper to taste.
  • 8 corn tortillas.

Garnishes

  • Shredded lettuce.
  • Chopped tomatoes.
  • 4 tablespoons of Shredded Cheddar-Monterrey Jack cheese blend.
  • Salsa.

Instructions

  1. Preppare mashed potatoes according to package directions.
  2. Heat oil in skillet over medium heat. Sautee diced onions in oil until tender and traslucent. Add cooked mashed potatoes and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Place tortillas on a plate. Heat in microwave until pliable, 15 to 20 seconds.
  4. Place 1/4 cup of potato mixture in each tortilla, fold in and secure each end with toothpicks.
  5. Heat oil in a skillet until shimmering. Fry each taco until crispy and golden brown, about 1 minute on each side.
  6. Remove toothpicks and top with garnishes.

Enjoy!
