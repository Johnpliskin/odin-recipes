Chicken Parm Sub!

In the time it takes to pick up take out and bring it home, you can have warm chicken Parmesan sandwiches ready for your family to enjoy. Serve with green salad.

What you need!

  • Cooking spray.
  • 4 frozen breaded chicken breast fillets.
  • Half a cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese.
  • 1 package of frozen garlic bread, thawed of course.
  • 1 cup of tomato sauce or marinara sauce.
  • 4 slices of provolone cheese.
  • 12 fresh basil leaves, or to taste.
  • Aluminum foil.

How to make it!

  1. Preheat the oven to 220ยบ celsius. Spray a baking pan with vegetable oil spray.
  2. Place the frozen chicken breasts on the preheated pan, and bake for 10 to 12 minutes. Turn the chicken, sprinkle 2 tablespoons of Parmesan cheese on each fillet, and bake until crispy, an additional 10 to 12 minutes.
  3. Slice garlic bread into 4 equal portions. Open each portion and place the garlic side up on a baking sheet. Warm the bread in the oven for about 5 minutes.
  4. To assemble the sandwiches, place a chicken fillet on a piece of garlic bread, spoon 1/4 cup tomato sauce or marinara sauce on top of the chicken, and lay a slice of provolone on top of the sauce. Place 3 basil leaves, or more to taste, on each sandwich, and top with the other half.
  5. wrap each sandwich in foil and return to the oven until the cheese is melted, 5 o 8 minutes. Unwrap and serve warm.
